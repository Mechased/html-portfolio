About Me

Hey there! I'm Maya, and let me tell you, I have a serious obsession with puns. They're like little bursts of wordplay sunshine that brighten my day. Sometimes they land perfectly, eliciting groans (the good kind!) from my friends and colleagues. Other times...well, let's just say my attempts are best left unsaid. But hey, even a failed pun is a pun in my book!

Speaking of books, I'm a total bookworm. I can devour a novel in a single sitting, especially if it's a fantasy adventure filled with dragons, hidden kingdoms, and plucky heroes. There's something magical about getting swept away to another world, living through the characters' triumphs and heartbreaks. Plus, who doesn't love a good escape from reality every now and then?

Oh, and did I mention I can knit a mean scarf? It all started with a dropped stitch and a YouTube tutorial, and now I'm a full-blown yarn enthusiast. There's something incredibly therapeutic about the rhythmic click-clack of needles and the growing warmth of a hand-knitted creation.
